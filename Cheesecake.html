No Bake Stawberry Cheesecake

Description

This fast, easy, and delicious no bake strawberry cheese cake will have you making 3 at a time! Cheesecakes can be modified in many ways to fit whatever you're in the mood for! My personal favorite is strawberries and I use them frozen and thaw them a half hour before I make the cheesecake. Blueberries, rasberries, peaches, and vanilla all work splendidly! Toppings alone are so fun to play with. You can add Oreo crumbs, sliced fruit, or chocolate shavings. I recommend adding blueberries and sliced strawberries to the top of this recipe!

Ingredients

  • Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • Cream Cheese
  • Butter
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Strawberries
  • Whipped Cream

Steps

  1. Grease a 9in pan.
  2. Begin with the crust: Mix together your butter, sugar, and salt to the graham cracker crumbs in a large bowl.
  3. Move the now mixed crumbs into your pan and press it fimly to the bottom and sides. Place in freezer.
  4. Preferably using a blender, combine your cream cheese, sugar, and strawberries until smooth. Move to a large bowl and fold your whipped cream in using a rubber spatula until combined evenly and is creamy.
  5. Remove your crust from the freezer. Using the same spatula, carefully move your strawberry filling into the crust and spread evenly to all the sides.
  6. Cover and chill for at least 4 hours.
