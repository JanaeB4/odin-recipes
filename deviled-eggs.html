Deviled Eggs

Description

Deviled eggs are a very popular side dish to brings to parties or to enjoy as a snack. Deviled eggs are fairly customizable, ranging from different toppings like bacon to changing the texture of the filling by adding pickles, chilies or avocados.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • Paprika
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Pickles
  • Hot Sauce of Choice (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil Eggs and deshell them.
  2. Grab a medium sized bowl. Cut your eggs in halves and place the yokes in the bowl. Arrange your boiled whites on a plate so they can be filled.
  3. Mash your yokes with a fork and add about 1 1/2 to 2 cups of mayo in your bowl as well as 1 tbsp of mustard. Mix very well making sure its smoothe.
  4. Dice 1 pickle and add it to your yoke mixture. Salt and pepper to taste an combine well.
  5. Grab a tall glass and a plastic or ziplock baggy. Sick a corner of your baggy into the bottom of your cup and fill the baggy with your yoke mix. Remove the bag and start twisting the open end closed so the yoke mix is being pushed to one corner. Cut the tip of the filled corner, best way to make a piping bag! add in a piping tip for different textures.
  6. Fill each egg white on your plate evenly and dust with paprika!
